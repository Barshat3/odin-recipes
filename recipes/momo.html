Momo Recipe

Ingredients Required:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for dough)
  • Water (as needed for dough)
  • 250g minced meat (chicken, pork, or buffalo) or vegetables
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2–3 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tomato achar (sauce) for serving

Steps to follow:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour with water and knead until smooth to form dough. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix minced meat or vegetables with onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, coriander, soy sauce, oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into thin circles (about 3 inches in diameter).
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  5. Fold and pleat the edges to seal the momo (use any folding style you like).
  6. Arrange momos in a steamer tray, making sure they don’t touch.
  7. Steam over boiling water for 8–10 minutes until the wrappers turn slightly translucent and the filling is cooked.
  8. Serve hot with tomato achar or your favorite dipping sauce.
