Salad Recipe

Ingredients Required:

  • 2 cups lettuce or mixed greens
  • 1 medium cucumber, sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn (boiled)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (mint, parsley, or coriander) for garnish

Steps to follow:

  1. Wash and drain all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop lettuce, cucumber, tomato, onion, carrot, and bell peppers into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a large salad bowl, combine all the vegetables and sweet corn.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat evenly.
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs.
  7. Serve immediately for best freshness.
