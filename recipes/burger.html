Burger Recipe

Ingridents Required:

  • Burger bun (top & bottom halves)
  • Beef patty (or chicken/veggie patty)
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (raw or caramelized)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & pepper (for seasoning the patty)
  • Cooking oil or butter (for grilling)

Steps to follow:

  1. Season the patty with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat and add oil or butter.
  3. Cook the patty for about 3â€“4 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Lightly toast the burger buns on the pan or grill.
  6. Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard on the buns as desired.
  7. Place lettuce on the bottom bun, followed by tomato slices and onion.
  8. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the vegetables.
  9. Top with pickles if desired.
  10. Close with the top bun and serve immediately.
